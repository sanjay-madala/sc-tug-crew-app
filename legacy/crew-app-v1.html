Demo Walkthrough

Tug RS 33 — Captain Somchai assists MARAN ARTEMIS (LNG carrier, 336m) berthing at PTTLNG LMPT1. 5 Large tugs + 1 standby required.

Steps

1. Tap MARAN ARTEMIS on home screen to open the job
2. Review vessel info (IMO, LOA 336m, GRT 161K, Greece flag) and tug group (5+1)
3. Tap Crew Check-In & Pre-Departure button
4. Tap all 4 crew cards to confirm aboard, then check all 3 items, then tap Confirm
5. Tap Departed — GPS auto-captured, tug leaves base
6. Tap Arrived at Station — at PTTLNG terminal
7. Select delay reason "Vessel late", then tap Standby
8. Tap Made Fast — tow lines connected to MARAN ARTEMIS
9. Tap Operation Active — pushing vessel to berth
10. Tap Let Go — lines released, vessel secured at berth
11. Tap Job Complete — see summary with all timestamps, photos, and notes
